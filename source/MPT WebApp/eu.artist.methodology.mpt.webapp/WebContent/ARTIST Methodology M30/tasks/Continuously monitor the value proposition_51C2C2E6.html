Task: VALPRO.A4
This task is focused on establishing the means to monitor continuously the value proposition in order to provide the customer with the maximum added value. This added value shall be aligned to his expectations and needs at all times. Several approaches can be followed for this: interviews, surveys, satisfaction questionnaires, workshops, analysis of the competitors, etc
