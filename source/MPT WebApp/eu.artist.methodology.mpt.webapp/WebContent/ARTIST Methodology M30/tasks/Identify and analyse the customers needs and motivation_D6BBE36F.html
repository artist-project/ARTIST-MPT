Task: VALPRO.A1
The value proposition must be aligned with the needs of the customers, their motivation and perspectives when buying the service. The identification of these needs can be done by interviews, surveys or observation. The same methods can be applied to the identification of the problems that need to be solved. The value proposition definition must explain what the added value of this product or service is with respect to its competitor, as it is a sales argument.
