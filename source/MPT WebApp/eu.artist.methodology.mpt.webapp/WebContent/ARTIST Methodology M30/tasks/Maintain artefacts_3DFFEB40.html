Task: REUSEPUB.A3
After an artefact has been published it will be used by different developers in different contexts. Possible errors in the artefact might be discovered and new requirements may come up. The owner of an artefact has to monitor the feedback received via the ARTIST Repository and Marketplace and act on it either by providing support and answering questions or by providing updated and improved versions of the artefact.
