Task: EMREQ.A1
The modeller browses his/her local workspace or the ARTIST repository (team migration) looking for the non-cloud PIM he/she wants to express requirements on. Requirements can be expressed on different views (concerns) of the non-cloud model (i.e. business logic, data storage, presentation, etc.).
Iterate this activity over the different models so as to express the requirements in all views.
