Task: BUSFEAS.A3
The goal of this activity is to determine the economic feasibility of the migration measured in terms of costs, benefits as well as other key metrics necessary for taking a decision. This analysis shall be performed in various steps. In a first step, the analyst shall estimate the economic figures of what a migration from scratch would cost. A second step consists of inserting these same data considering the recommendations provided by the MAT and the effort estimated by the TFT.
