Task: REUSEID.A2
The reuse potential of the candidate artefacts is further explored by looking at their level of abstraction, the usefulness for typical development tasks and the application domain that they cover. The optionally developer checks, if the artefacts can be made more general before publication. In addition the reuse potential the decision whether to publish an artefact can depend also on the IPR relevant knowledge contained in the artefact and other organisational constraints. These factors also influence the decision whether to make an artefact publicly available via the ARTIST Marketplace or to limit access to the artefact by publishing it to a project in the ARTIST Repository.
