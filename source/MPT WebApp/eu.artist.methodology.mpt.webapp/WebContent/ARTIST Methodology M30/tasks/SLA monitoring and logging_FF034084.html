Task: CLODEPAVAUD.A2
The main purpose of this activity is to receive the information on the SLA compliant resources from CLODEPAVAUD.A1 and log their status continuously, based again on the way this status is defined by the respective provider. The statistical data are stored in a MongoDB NoSQL database, for usage in CLODEPAVAUD.A3.
