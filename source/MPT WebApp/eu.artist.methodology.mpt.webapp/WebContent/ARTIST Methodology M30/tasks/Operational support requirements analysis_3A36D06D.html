Task: TECHEVA.A2
The Operational support requirements analysis objective is to study and analyse the requirements (from the application and system point of view) required to perform operational activities needed to carry out the activity of the company. For this, operational support components are required as part of the application. Operational support components encompass the set of operational related management capabilities required in order to manage and control the cloud services offered to customers. They include:
- Monitoring and reporting
- Service policy management
- Service Level management auditing
- Incident & problem management
