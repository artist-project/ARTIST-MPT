Task: REUSEID.A1
The newly produced modelling artefacts in a migration project are periodically reviewed to determine, if they are potentially reusable by other teams or projects. Candidates for reusable artefacts are especially more abstract artefacts such as meta-models or UML profiles. The activity provides a first list of artefacts to be further investigated.
