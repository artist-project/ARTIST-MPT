Task: VERBEUBT.A6
After both traces have been brought to a platform independent level they can be compared to yield a verdict. A positive verdict (pass) is yielded when both traces are equal in terms of methods executed and/or output produced, and a negative verdict (fail) is yielded when the traces are distinct. Since some user requests might depend upon each other it might be necessary to stop testing the whole user session as this would otherwise produce failures provoked by a previously detected failure.
