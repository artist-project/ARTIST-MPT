Task: OPERATIONAL.A2
In order for a SaaS application to run, some key infrastructure resources need to be identified. These resources can be distinguished between infrastructure and 3rd party services:
- The infrastructure resources can be either related to a public cloud or on a private one. It is important to identify the target cloud provider and their offer. In the case a public provider is selected, the pricing model of the target provider must be collected. In addition to that, a mechanism to account the cloud provider must be updated / created. In the case a private provider is selected, the company must consider the operational costs of running such an infrastructure (personnel, licenses, hardware, etc.)
- 3rd party services (e.g. monitoring components, billing components, etc) and the functionalities provided by them must be determined. The pricing model of these components must also be analysed and identified. Thus a mechanism to account for these components need to be defined.
