Task: IMRP.A5
In order to prioritize the incidences it is required to take into account both the urgency of the incidence (how quickly the business needs a resolution) and the level of impact it is causing. Factors that should take into account for determining the impact:
- The number of services affected – may be multiple services
- Effect on business reputation
- Regulatory or legislative breaches.
It should be noted that an incidence´s priority may be dynamic – if circumstances change, or if an incidence is not resolved within SLA target times, then the priority must be altered to reflect the new situation.
