Task: MODELUNDER.A1
Transform the previously obtained models into more refined ones, thus producing higher level views on the initially treated non-cloud artefacts. This is realized using different techniques such as model slicing for instance, implemented as model transformations. This sub-activity can be repeated several times consecutively until the expected/needed derived models are obtained
