Task: MP.A5
This activity will set in place control techniques for monitoring the marketing plan's performance. Usually this entails a systematic review of all aspects of the plan against targets set, usually on a monthly or quarterly basis. The review must be carried out regularly to ensure prompt attention and action in areas when the results lag behind targets set. Managers and others responsible for implementation of all elements of the action program should be involved in this activity. If during this activity it is discovered that marketing objectives are not the most appropriate ones, the process should be repeated from activity "Identify The marketing objectives"
