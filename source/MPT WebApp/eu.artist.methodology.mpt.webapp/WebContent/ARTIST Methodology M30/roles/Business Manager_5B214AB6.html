Role: Business Manager
A Business Manager is a person who drives the work of others in order to run a major business efficiently and make a large profit. He or she should have working knowledge of the following areas, and may be a specialist in one or more: sales, marketing, and public relations; economics; production; finance; accounting, auditing, tax, and budgeting; purchasing; and personnel.
